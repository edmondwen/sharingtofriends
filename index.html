About us!

We are a tradition chinese resturant that has been in the culinary business for over 40 years. From generation to generation we have devloped and always strive to bring great food to your family and friends. We bring fresh and traditional chinese food during this tough covid-19 times.

History about this place

The name Wong silkway is derived by the combination of the names Wong and Silkway who, in 1975, founded a successful traditional chinese restaurant located in the city of Beijing, China. It all started with Larry wong who was a pround fisherman that loved to cook in his spare time. He met his wife, susan silkway through a vacation and they created this resturant from Larry's seafood dishes and susan's irish tradtional comfort food. Now, a third generation of the Wong's family is bringing this famous and delicious Chinese Cuisine to Wong's silkway, matched with a fine selection of modern irish beer and deserts.
